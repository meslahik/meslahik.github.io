I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Lugano, where I am serving as a research assistant in Distributed Systems groups under the supervision of Prof. Fernando Pedone.

Research

My research interests include distributed data structures and dependable distributed systems.

I am currently working on using RDMA network to boost the performance of distributed systems. In particular, we are interested in using RDMA's shared-memory model to build fault-tolerant systems that are both scalable and strongly consistent.
Lately, I worked on a distributed B+Tree algorithm called DynaTree. DynaTree is a replicated B+Tree that provides scalable performance for a number of workloads while ensuring linearizability.
